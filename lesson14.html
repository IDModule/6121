😊 البلد الذي يقيس السعادة | The country that measures happiness

مرحباً بك في الدرس الرابع عشر! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن بوتان، البلد الذي يقيس السعادة الوطنية الإجمالية.

Welcome to Lesson 14! In this lesson, we'll learn about Bhutan, the country that measures Gross National Happiness.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
happiness السعادة Happiness is more important than wealth.
GDP (Gross Domestic Product) الناتج المحلي الإجمالي GDP measures a country's economic output.
Bhutan بوتان Bhutan is a small country in the Himalayas.
well-being الرفاهية The government is concerned about citizens' well-being.
measure يقيس How do you measure success?
index مؤشر The happiness index ranks countries by their citizens' happiness.
GNH (Gross National Happiness) السعادة الوطنية الإجمالية Bhutan uses GNH instead of GDP.
sustainable مستدام Bhutan focuses on sustainable development.
policy سياسة The government has a policy to promote happiness.
indicator مؤشر Health is an important indicator of well-being.
prosperity الازدهار Economic prosperity doesn't always lead to happiness.
quality of life جودة الحياة The quality of life is high in Scandinavian countries.
contentment الرضا Contentment comes from within, not from material possessions.
survey استطلاع The government conducts an annual happiness survey.
priority أولوية In Bhutan, happiness is a national priority.
balance توازن Finding a balance between work and leisure is important for happiness.
