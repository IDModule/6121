🗣️ تعلم لغات متعددة | Learning multiple languages

مرحباً بك في الدرس التاسع! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن فوائد وتحديات تعلم لغات متعددة.

Welcome to Lesson 9! In this lesson, we'll learn about the benefits and challenges of learning multiple languages.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
multilingual متعدد اللغات She is multilingual and speaks five languages.
native language اللغة الأم Arabic is his native language.
fluency الطلاقة She speaks English with fluency.
cognitive skills المهارات المعرفية Learning languages improves cognitive skills.
bilingual ثنائي اللغة Many children grow up bilingual.
vocabulary المفردات He has an extensive vocabulary in French.
accent اللهجة She speaks with a British accent.
immersion الانغماس Language immersion is an effective learning method.
proficiency الإتقان His proficiency in Spanish is impressive.
linguistics علم اللغويات She studied linguistics at university.
dialect اللهجة المحلية Each region has its own dialect.
mother tongue اللغة الأم English is not his mother tongue.
polyglot متعدد اللغات A polyglot is someone who speaks several languages.
acquisition اكتساب Language acquisition is easier for children.
translation الترجمة The translation of the book was excellent.
interpretation الترجمة الفورية She works as an interpreter at conferences.
