👃 لماذا تجعلنا الروائح نشعر بأننا في المنزل | Why smells make us feel at home

مرحباً بك في الدرس السادس عشر! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن كيفية ارتباط الروائح بالذكريات والشعور بالانتماء للمنزل.

Welcome to Lesson 16! In this lesson, we'll learn about how smells are connected to memories and the feeling of being at home.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
smell الرائحة The smell of fresh bread reminds me of my childhood.
memory الذاكرة Smells can trigger powerful memories.
home المنزل Certain smells make us feel at home.
nostalgia الحنين إلى الوطن The smell of her grandmother's cooking filled her with nostalgia.
olfactory شمي The olfactory system is responsible for our sense of smell.
trigger يثير Certain smells can trigger emotional responses.
association ارتباط We form strong associations between smells and experiences.
evoke يستحضر Certain smells can evoke childhood memories.
familiar مألوف The familiar smell of coffee made her feel comfortable.
comfort الراحة The smell of fresh laundry brings comfort to many people.
scent العطر The scent of roses filled the garden.
aroma الرائحة الزكية The aroma of fresh coffee woke her up.
fragrance العطر The fragrance of flowers filled the room.
emotional عاطفي Smells can create strong emotional responses.
sensation إحساس The sensation of smelling fresh bread is comforting.
reminiscent مذكر The smell was reminiscent of her childhood home.
