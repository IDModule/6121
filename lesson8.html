🤖 هل يمكن للذكاء الاصطناعي حل الجرائم؟ | Can AI solve crime?

مرحباً بك في الدرس الثامن! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن دور الذكاء الاصطناعي في حل الجرائم والتحديات المرتبطة به.

Welcome to Lesson 8! In this lesson, we'll learn about the role of AI in solving crimes and the challenges associated with it.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
artificial intelligence (AI) الذكاء الاصطناعي AI is changing how police solve crimes.
crime الجريمة The police are investigating the crime.
data البيانات AI systems analyze large amounts of data.
predict يتنبأ AI can predict where crimes might occur.
algorithm خوارزمية The algorithm analyzes patterns in criminal behavior.
surveillance المراقبة Surveillance cameras help identify suspects.
facial recognition التعرف على الوجه Facial recognition technology can identify people in crowds.
privacy الخصوصية Using AI in policing raises privacy concerns.
bias التحيز AI systems may have bias in their algorithms.
evidence الدليل The police collected evidence from the crime scene.
analyze يحلل AI can analyze listening footage quickly.
pattern نمط The system looks for patterns in criminal behavior.
ethical أخلاقي There are ethical concerns about using AI in policing.
database قاعدة بيانات The police have a database of fingerprints.
technology التكنولوجيا Modern technology helps solve complex crimes.
accuracy الدقة The accuracy of AI systems is improving.
