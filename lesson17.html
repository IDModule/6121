🎵 أن تصبح موسيقياً | Becoming a musician

مرحباً بك في الدرس السابع عشر! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن كيفية أن تصبح موسيقياً وما يتطلبه ذلك من مهارات وتدريب.

Welcome to Lesson 17! In this lesson, we'll learn about becoming a musician and what skills and training it requires.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
musician موسيقي She is a talented musician who plays three instruments.
instrument آلة موسيقية The piano is a beautiful instrument to learn.
practice التدريب You need to practice every day to become a good musician.
talent الموهبة She has a natural talent for music.
dedication التفاني Becoming a professional musician requires dedication.
perform يؤدي She will perform at the concert next week.
concert حفلة موسيقية We attended a classical music concert last night.
compose يؤلف Mozart began to compose music at a very young age.
melody لحن The melody of that song is beautiful.
rhythm إيقاع She has a good sense of rhythm.
harmony تناغم The harmony between the different instruments was perfect.
orchestra أوركسترا She plays violin in the national orchestra.
band فرقة موسيقية He plays guitar in a rock band.
rehearsal بروفة The band has a rehearsal every Tuesday evening.
technique تقنية Her piano technique has improved a lot.
passion شغف His passion for music is inspiring.
