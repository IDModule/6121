🫒 أي زيت طبخ هو الأفضل؟ | Which cooking oil is the best?

مرحباً بك في الدرس الرابع! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن أنواع زيوت الطبخ المختلفة وأيها الأفضل للصحة.

Welcome to Lesson 4! In this lesson, we'll learn about different types of cooking oils and which ones are best for health.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
cooking oil زيت الطبخ Which cooking oil do you prefer for frying?
smoke point نقطة الدخان The smoke point determines how hot you can heat the oil.
saturated fat دهون مشبعة Coconut oil is high in saturated fat.
olive oil زيت الزيتون Extra virgin olive oil is great for salads.
refined مكرر Refined oils have a higher smoke point.
unrefined غير مكرر Unrefined oils retain more nutrients.
monounsaturated أحادي غير مشبع Monounsaturated fats are considered healthy.
polyunsaturated متعدد غير مشبع Sunflower oil contains polyunsaturated fats.
deep frying القلي العميق Deep frying requires oils with high smoke points.
antioxidants مضادات الأكسدة Extra virgin olive oil is rich in antioxidants.
cholesterol كوليسترول Plant oils don't contain cholesterol.
heart health صحة القلب Olive oil is beneficial for heart health.
inflammation التهاب Some oils can reduce inflammation in the body.
processing معالجة Heavy processing can remove nutrients from oils.
flavor profile نكهة مميزة Each oil has its own unique flavor profile.
versatile متعدد الاستخدامات Canola oil is very versatile for cooking.
