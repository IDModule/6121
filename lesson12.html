🥗 وجبات صحية بميزانية محدودة | Healthy meals on a budget

مرحباً بك في الدرس الثاني عشر! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن كيفية تحضير وجبات صحية بميزانية محدودة.

Welcome to Lesson 12! In this lesson, we'll learn about how to prepare healthy meals on a budget.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
budget الميزانية We need to stick to our food budget this month.
meal planning التخطيط للوجبات Meal planning helps save money on groceries.
fast food الوجبات السريعة Fast food is often more expensive than home-cooked meals.
savings التوفير Cooking at home leads to significant savings.
ingredients المكونات Buy ingredients in bulk to save money.
nutritious مغذي Beans are both cheap and nutritious.
affordable بأسعار معقولة Seasonal vegetables are more affordable.
grocery shopping التسوق للبقالة Make a list before grocery shopping.
leftovers بقايا الطعام You can use leftovers to make new meals.
bulk buying الشراء بكميات كبيرة Bulk buying can save money in the long run.
seasonal produce المنتجات الموسمية Seasonal produce is fresher and cheaper.
discount خصم Look for discounts when shopping for groceries.
meal prep تحضير الوجبات مسبقاً Sunday is my meal prep day for the week.
cost-effective فعال من حيث التكلفة Cooking at home is more cost-effective than eating out.
economical اقتصادي Beans and rice make an economical meal.
shopping list قائمة التسوق Always make a shopping list before going to the store.
