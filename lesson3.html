🍿 الأطعمة المقرمشة | Foods that go crunch

مرحباً بك في الدرس الثالث! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن الأطعمة المقرمشة وتأثيرها على صحتنا النفسية والجسدية.

Welcome to Lesson 3! In this lesson, we'll learn about crunchy foods and their effects on our mental and physical health.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
crunch قرمشة/صوت المضغ I love the crunch of fresh apples.
texture نسيج/ملمس The texture of this bread is perfect.
satisfying مُرضي/مُشبع Eating crunchy foods is very satisfying.
stress relief تخفيف التوتر Crunchy foods provide stress relief.
crispy مقرمش I prefer crispy vegetables to soft ones.
chewing مضغ Chewing crunchy foods exercises your jaw muscles.
appetite شهية Crunchy snacks can control your appetite.
nutritious مغذي Raw carrots are both crunchy and nutritious.
mindful eating الأكل الواعي Crunchy foods encourage mindful eating.
sensory experience تجربة حسية Eating is a complete sensory experience.
fiber ألياف Crunchy vegetables are high in fiber.
snack وجبة خفيفة Nuts make a healthy crunchy snack.
jaw muscles عضلات الفك Crunchy foods strengthen your jaw muscles.
psychological نفسي There are psychological benefits to eating crunchy foods.
portion control التحكم في الحصص Crunchy foods help with portion control.
fresh produce المنتجات الطازجة Fresh produce provides the best crunch.
