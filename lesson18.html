💼 ما هو مستقبل العمل؟ | What is the future of work?

مرحباً بك في الدرس الثامن عشر! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن مستقبل العمل والتغييرات التي قد تحدث في سوق العمل.

Welcome to Lesson 18! In this lesson, we'll learn about the future of work and the changes that may occur in the job market.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
work العمل The future of work is changing rapidly.
future المستقبل What will jobs look like in the future?
artificial intelligence الذكاء الاصطناعي Artificial intelligence will change many industries.
skills المهارات Workers need to develop new skills for the future.
automation الأتمتة Automation will replace some jobs but create others.
remote work العمل عن بعد Remote work has become more common since the pandemic.
flexible مرن Many companies now offer flexible working hours.
technology التكنولوجيا Technology is changing how we work.
adapt يتكيف Workers must adapt to new technologies.
digital رقمي Digital skills are essential in today's job market.
workforce القوى العاملة The workforce is becoming more diverse.
innovation الابتكار Innovation drives economic growth.
collaborate يتعاون Teams can collaborate online from different locations.
productivity الإنتاجية Technology can increase productivity in the workplace.
career المسار المهني People may have multiple careers in their lifetime.
upskill تطوير المهارات Workers need to upskill to remain employable.
