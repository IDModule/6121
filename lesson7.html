مرحباً بك في الدرس السابع! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن تأثير تغير المناخ على أنماط هجرة الحيوانات.

Welcome to Lesson 7! In this lesson, we'll learn about how climate change is affecting animal migration patterns.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
migration الهجرة Bird migration is affected by climate change.
climate change تغير المناخ Climate change is altering ecosystems worldwide.
habitat الموطن Many animals are losing their natural habitats.
species الأنواع Some species cannot adapt quickly enough.
adapt يتكيف Animals must adapt to changing conditions.
seasonal موسمي Seasonal patterns are becoming less predictable.
disruption اضطراب Climate change causes disruption to migration patterns.
temperature درجة الحرارة Rising temperatures affect animal behavior.
breeding التكاثر Breeding seasons are changing due to climate change.
impact تأثير The impact of climate change on wildlife is significant.
pattern نمط Migration patterns are changing rapidly.
survival البقاء على قيد الحياة Survival depends on adapting to new conditions.
timing التوقيت The timing of migrations is shifting earlier.
route المسار Birds are changing their migration routes.
ecosystem النظام البيئي Ecosystems are affected by changing migration patterns.
conservation الحفاظ على Conservation efforts are needed to protect migratory species.
