مرحباً بك في الدرس العاشر! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن الحساسية وأسبابها وأعراضها.

Welcome to Lesson 10! In this lesson, we'll learn about allergies, their causes, and symptoms.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
allergy الحساسية She has a severe peanut allergy.
immune system جهاز المناعة The immune system protects the body from infections.
allergen مسببات الحساسية Pollen is a common allergen in spring.
symptoms الأعراض Common allergy symptoms include sneezing and itchy eyes.
reaction رد فعل She had a severe allergic reaction to the medication.
pollen حبوب اللقاح Tree pollen causes allergies in many people.
dust mite عث الغبار Dust mites are a common cause of allergies.
antihistamine مضاد الهيستامين She takes antihistamines to control her allergies.
intolerance عدم التحمل Lactose intolerance is different from a milk allergy.
trigger محفز Certain foods can trigger allergic reactions.
diagnosis التشخيص The doctor made a diagnosis of seasonal allergies.
chronic مزمن She suffers from chronic allergies all year round.
inflammation التهاب Allergies can cause inflammation in the airways.
sensitivity الحساسية He has a sensitivity to certain chemicals.
exposure التعرض Repeated exposure to allergens can worsen symptoms.
prevention الوقاية Prevention is the best approach for managing allergies.
