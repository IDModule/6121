🐄 هل يمكن للأبقار منع حرائق الغابات؟ | Can cows prevent wildfires?

مرحباً بك في الدرس السادس! في هذا الدرس، سنتعلم عن دور الأبقار في منع حرائق الغابات والحفاظ على البيئة.

Welcome to Lesson 6! In this lesson, we'll learn about the role of cows in preventing wildfires and protecting the environment.

الكلمة | Word النطق | Pronunciation الترجمة | Translation مثال | Example
wildfire حرائق الغابات Wildfires are becoming more common due to climate change.
grazing الرعي Grazing animals help maintain grasslands.
prevention الوقاية Prevention is better than cure.
vegetation الغطاء النباتي Dense vegetation can fuel wildfires.
fuel وقود Dry grass can fuel a wildfire.
ecosystem النظام البيئي Cows play an important role in the ecosystem.
sustainable مستدام We need sustainable solutions to environmental problems.
management إدارة Proper land management can prevent wildfires.
reduce يقلل Grazing can reduce wildfire risk.
landscape المناظر الطبيعية Cows help shape the landscape.
controlled مُسيطر عليه Controlled grazing is beneficial for the environment.
impact تأثير Cows have a positive impact on fire prevention.
strategy استراتيجية Using cows is a natural strategy for wildfire prevention.
benefit فائدة The benefits of grazing include wildfire prevention.
environment البيئة We must protect our environment.
natural طبيعي Cows provide a natural solution to wildfire prevention.
